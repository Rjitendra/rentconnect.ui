Pricing

We offer you 2 months of no-commitment trial for free, without any credit card requirements. Sign up with Stripe for receiving rent.

Free 2-Months

Get 2 months free trial after sign-up. No credit card needed. You'll be asked to pay if you wish to continue using the services beyond two months.

All the features are free to use except rent collection, which will have its gateway and management charges.

$5/month After Trial

After your two months trial, pay $60 yearly (just $5/month) for all of the services (billed annually). Even if you renew early, your paid subscription will start after 60 days of free use.

You'll receive reminders in due course. Non-active accounts stay with us, but in dormant stage.

2% Rent Charge

All online rent payment/collection has a flat 2%+ Stripe gateway charge towards managing the financial transactions. This is charged during the trial period as well.

You can see the latest Stripe charges here. You can enter the cash transactions in the system yourself for no charge.
